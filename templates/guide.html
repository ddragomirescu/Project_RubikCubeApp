Beginner's Guide

Welcome to the beginner's guide for solving the Rubik's Cube! This guide will walk you through the fundamental concepts and steps needed to solve a standard 3x3 Rubik's Cube. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refresh your knowledge, this guide will help you understand the basic principles of cube solving.

Before we begin, familiarize yourself with the basic notation:

  • R - Right face clockwise
  • L - Left face clockwise
  • U - Upper face clockwise
  • D - Down face clockwise
  • F - Front face clockwise
  • B - Back face clockwise
  • R' - Right face counter-clockwise (and similarly for others)

Step 1: White Cross

The first step in solving the Rubik's Cube is to create a white cross on one face. This involves positioning the four white edge pieces around the white center piece.
